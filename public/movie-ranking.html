The Best Movies According to Matt

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Catch Me If You Can

I love this movie because it is amazing to me how genius the main character is at being a con.

Forrest Gump

It's another Tom Hanks Movie. It illustrates the experience of a small town man, oblivious to the wonderous experiences he encounters.

Cast Away

The film shows that one can overcome so much adversity and desolation, only with a little bit of hope and love.
